Brands


There are many different brands of mountain bike available from the most expensive Rocky Mountain to the cheap option Trek. There is a bike for everyone.


Best brands of 2021


These are the best mountain biking brands for 2021 according to switchbacktravel.com.


  • Specilized - Quality, proven bikes for every type of rider at nearly every price point.
  • Trek - No-nonsense company with an expansive range of models and build kits; lifetime warranty on their frames.
  • Santa Cruz - Boutique feel, top-quality frames, and standout attention to detail. Lifetime warranty on frames as well as pivot bearings.
  • Giant Bicycles - Extensive manufacturing experience and very competitive prices.
  • Cannondale - Not afraid to do things a bit different; lightweight aluminum frames.
  • Yeti Cycles - Highly desirable, fantastic-looking bikes that climb better than most.
  • Pivot Cycles - DW link suspension design is efficient on the climbs yet very capable on the descents.
  • Ibis Cycles - Quality bikes with relatively reasonable pricing compared to their competition.
  • Evil Bikes Co. - Focus on having fun and excellent downhill performance.
  • Kona - A no-frills company with competitive pricing that prioritizes fun over all else.
  • YT industries - Direct-to-consumer model delivers amazing bang for your buck.
